Chapter III

What the heavens are

Heaven is a perfect sphere; nothing in it is generated or corrupted. Its rays descend to the earth at the centre, and these are the source of the virtue of images. Each planet moves things of its own quality — and the Sun draws that virtue out and increases it.

    Chapter IV · The lunar dwellings

    The 28 Mansions of the Moon

    The zodiac is divided into twenty-eight equal arcs of 12°51′25.7″, beginning at 0° Aries. The Moon's mansion governs which images may be made. Select one to light it on the dial. (Operations are condensed summaries, not the translators' wording.)

    Chapter IV · The rule of election

    Placing the Moon well

    The Moon governs every work beneath her, so she must be well placed. Keep the benefic column for good works; the text says do the contrary for works of harm.

    How it calculates

    • Positions: Schlyter analytic ephemeris — geocentric, tropical, of date (~1–4′).
    • Mansions: 360 ÷ 28 from 0° Aries.
    • Dignities, aspects, combustion, Via Combusta: after W. Lilly, Christian Astrology (1647).
    • Ascendant: standard RAMC formula (approximate).
